Frequently Aked Questions

Q:How late do you take calls?

A:nothing past 9pm please

Q:How much do you charge per walk??

A:We charge .37 cents per step. We calculate this by dipping your pup's paws in ink (gluten free ink is available) and calculating the amount of steps per walk with a clicker.

Q:Do you take cards?

A:No. We only take cash and checks. All fractions of a cent shall be rounded up

Q:What should I do if I want my dog walked but you are not home

A:All dogs under 40kg can be dispenced into the doggy shute. All dogs over 40kg must use the doggy door

Q:Are the dogs walked in groups or alone?

A:We generally walk them in groups of 3-5

Q:My dog does not get along with other dogs, but still needs to be walked. What are my options?

A:You may charter a solo walk for a premium price, unfortunately as we are very busy solo walks are more difficult to book

Q:Can I get some sort of assurance of the quality of these walks? What does a dog look like after it has been walked by your service
